the maker

by Cianna Rose is designed and sewn by Cianna herself — every seam matched, every plaid lined up across the center front, pocket, and sleeves.

the materials

100% cotton, always. Yarn-dyed poplin for structure, pointelle knit for breathability, and a soft-washed finish so everything feels broken-in from the first wear.

the details

Woven labels, satin-stitch embroidery, lettuce-edge hems, and self-fabric ties. The small things you notice at 2am are the things we obsess over at the sewing table.
